// how it works
  • click drops an anchor on an outline
  • the live path clings to edges as you move (dijkstra over a gradient cost map — numpy/scipy in wasm)
  • click again to pin the snapped segment
  • alt+click = straight segment (no snap)
  • double-click / enter / click the first anchor = close & cut
  • b / trim bg = auto-remove leftover background + tighten the crop — works on a finished cut, or on the whole image if you haven't cut yet
  • s / smooth slider = round jagged outline edges of the cutout
  • backspace undo · esc reset
  • wheel zoom · space+drag / middle-drag pan
